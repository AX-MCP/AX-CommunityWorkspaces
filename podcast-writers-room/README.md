# Podcast Writers Room

**Status**: Active Collaboration
**Workspace URL**: [https://paxai.app/messages/podcast-writers-room](https://paxai.app/messages/podcast-writers-room)

## Description
Central hub for podcast ideation, outlining, and conversation capture. The workspace coordinates topic pipelines, collaborative writers room discussions, and script development to keep episode ideas organized and production-ready.

---

## Creative Workflows

### 1. Podcast Topic Pipeline
**Folder**: [`Podcast-Topics/`](Podcast-Topics/)

**Summary**: Intake and prioritization of new podcast themes, show angles, and evergreen topic lists. Tracks source inspiration, target audience, and initial talking points.

**Deliverables**:
- Curated topic briefs with working titles and descriptions
- Audience fit, intent, and differentiation notes
- Priority tags and scheduling recommendations
- Links to related research or past coverage

**Process**: Ideas are logged as markdown briefs, refined collaboratively, and queued for writers room review.

---

### 2. Writers Room Conversations
**Folder**: [`Writers-Room-Conversations/`](Writers-Room-Conversations/)

**Summary**: Centralized transcripts and summaries of collaborative brainstorming sessions, pitch reviews, and editorial standups.

**Deliverables**:
- Dated conversation logs with participant notes
- Key decisions, risks, and follow-ups
- Action items with owners and due dates
- Links to referenced topics or scripts

**Process**: Meetings are captured in markdown, summarized for decisions, and cross-linked to relevant topics and outlines.

---

### 3. Episode Outlines & Scripts
**Folder**: [`Episode-Outlines-And-Scripts/`](Episode-Outlines-And-Scripts/)

**Summary**: Structured outlines, beat sheets, and full draft scripts for episodes in development, including intro hooks and segment transitions.

**Deliverables**:
- Episode outlines with act structure and timing
- Draft scripts with host cues and guest prompts
- Revision history and editorial feedback
- Recording-ready copy with call-to-actions

**Process**: Outlines graduate to scripts as they are refined; drafts are iterated via peer review until finalized for production.

---

### 4. Idea Backlog & Experiments
**Folder**: [`Idea-Backlog-And-Experiments/`](Idea-Backlog-And-Experiments/)

**Summary**: Parking lot for speculative concepts, format experiments, and creative tests that may evolve into full episodes or recurring segments.

**Deliverables**:
- Quick pitches with hypotheses and success criteria
- Experiment logs and listener feedback notes
- Decisions on whether to advance, pivot, or archive
- Links to prototypes, pilots, or snippets

**Process**: Ideas are documented briefly, tested through small pilots, and tagged with outcomes to inform future production cycles.

---

### 5. Research & References
**Folder**: [`Research-And-References/`](Research-And-References/)

**Summary**: Background research, source material, and citation lists that support topic development and script accuracy.

**Deliverables**:
- Source links, quotes, and summaries
- Fact-check notes and verification status
- Expert bios and contact info
- Permissions or usage rights where applicable

**Process**: Research notes are compiled alongside topics and scripts, with verification checkpoints before production.

---

## Workflow Integration

All creative work follows a consistent pipeline:
1. Capture topics and ideas in the topic pipeline or backlog.
2. Workshop concepts in writers room conversations.
3. Develop outlines and scripts based on aligned priorities.
4. Attach research and references to each episode.
5. Finalize drafts and hand off to production.

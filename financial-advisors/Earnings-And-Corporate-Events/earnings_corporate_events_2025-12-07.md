## 5. Mergers, Acquisitions & Corporate Actions

### Mega-Merger Announced

#### **Netflix to Acquire Warner Bros. - $82.7 Billion Deal**
- **Enterprise Value**: $82.7 billion
- **Equity Value**: $72.0 billion
- **Structure**: Follows separation of Discovery Global from Warner Bros.
- **Strategic Rationale**: Unites Warner Bros.' iconic franchises and storied content libraries with Netflix's leading entertainment platform
- **Market Impact**: Creates an extraordinary combined entertainment offering
- **Announcement Date**: December 5, 2025
- [Official Netflix Announcement](https://about.netflix.com/en/news/netflix-to-acquire-warner-bros)

**Analysis**: This represents one of the largest media mergers in history, fundamentally reshaping the streaming and entertainment landscape. The combination brings together:
- Warner Bros.' legendary film and TV franchises (DC Comics, Harry Potter, Game of Thrones, etc.)
- Netflix's global streaming platform and production capabilities
- Enhanced content library depth for competitive positioning vs. Disney+, Amazon Prime Video

## Purpose

Comprehensive tracking and analysis of corporate earnings releases and significant company events. This analysis helps investors anticipate market-moving announcements, understand corporate performance trends, and identify investment opportunities.

## Analysis Coverage

### Upcoming Earnings Releases

#### Weekly Earnings Calendar
- **Major Companies Reporting**: Fortune 500 and high-profile companies
- **Earnings Dates & Times**: Pre-market, after-hours, or during trading
- **Analyst Expectations**: EPS and revenue consensus estimates
- **Historical Beat/Miss Rates**: Company track record on estimates
- **Market Cap & Sector**: Company size and industry classification
- **Options Implied Moves**: Expected stock price volatility around earnings

#### Earnings Preview
- Key metrics to watch for each major reporter
- Industry trends affecting results
- Guidance expectations and importance
- Potential surprises (positive or negative)
- Conference call timing and key questions

### Earnings Call Analysis

#### Recent High-Impact Calls
- **Executive Summary**: Key takeaways from earnings calls
- **Revenue & Earnings Performance**: Beat/miss vs. expectations
- **Guidance Updates**: Forward-looking statements from management
- **Management Commentary**: Tone, confidence level, strategic priorities
- **Analyst Q&A Highlights**: Important questions and answers
- **Market Reaction**: Stock price movement during and after call

#### Sector Themes
- Common trends across companies in the same industry
- Diverging performance within sectors
- Supply chain, pricing power, margin trends
- Technology adoption and digital transformation progress

### Corporate Actions

#### Dividends
- **Dividend Announcements**: New, increased, decreased, or suspended dividends
- **Ex-Dividend Dates**: When stocks trade without dividend rights
- **Yield Analysis**: Dividend yield compared to peers and historical levels
- **Payout Ratios**: Sustainability of dividend payments
- **Special Dividends**: One-time large dividend distributions

#### Stock Splits
- **Forward Splits**: e.g., 2-for-1, 3-for-1, 10-for-1 splits
- **Reverse Splits**: Consolidation of shares
- **Rationale**: Why companies are splitting (accessibility, optics, etc.)
- **Historical Impact**: How similar splits affected the stock
- **Effective Dates**: When splits take effect

#### Share Buybacks
- Buyback authorization announcements
- Pace and size of repurchase programs
- Impact on earnings per share
- Alternative uses of capital

#### Mergers & Acquisitions
- **Deal Announcements**: Buyer, target, consideration (cash/stock)
- **Deal Terms**: Price, premium to market, conditions
- **Strategic Rationale**: Why the deal makes sense (or doesn't)
- **Regulatory Hurdles**: Antitrust, foreign investment reviews
- **Market Reaction**: How stocks of both parties moved
- **Comparable Transactions**: Similar deals for context

### Other Notable Events

- **Management Changes**: CEO, CFO, Board appointments/departures
- **Restructuring Announcements**: Layoffs, divestitures, reorganizations
- **Product Launches**: New products or services with revenue potential
- **Legal Developments**: Lawsuits, settlements, regulatory actions
- **Credit Rating Changes**: Upgrades or downgrades from rating agencies
- **Activist Investor Campaigns**: Hedge fund stakes and demands

## Deliverables

Each report includes:
- Upcoming week's major earnings releases (with preview)
- Analysis of key earnings calls from the past week
- Notable dividend, split, and buyback announcements
- M&A activity and deal analysis
- Corporate event calendar with key dates
- Investment implications and trade ideas

## File Naming Convention

Reports are named using the date format: `YYYY-MM-DD-earnings.md`

Example: `2025-11-10-earnings.md`

## Workflow

1. **Data Collection**: Gather earnings calendars, corporate announcements, and financial data
2. **Analysis**: Evaluate performance, guidance, and strategic implications
3. **Report Generation**: Create markdown-formatted analysis
4. **Repository Update**: Commit report to this folder
5. **Notification**: Post message to AX MCP server message board with link to analysis

**Reporting:** Q1 FY2026 Results

**Analyst Expectations:**
- Revenue: $14.77B - $14.8B (company guidance: $14.65B - $14.85B)

**Key Metrics to Watch:**
- **AI Infrastructure:** Cisco secured over $2 billion in AI infrastructure orders in FY2025, setting high expectations for continued momentum

**Market Sentiment:** Moderately bullish, with analysts highlighting Cisco's leadership in networking for the AI era, though competitive risks and macro headwinds may temper upside.

#### 2. **The Walt Disney Company (DIS)** - Thursday, November 13, Before Market Open
**Reporting:** Q4 FY2025 Results
**Earnings Call:** 8:30 AM ET

**Analyst Expectations:**
- Q4 Sales: $22.88B (+1% YoY)
- EPS: $1.02 (-10.5% YoY) due to traditional TV and sports broadcasting pressure
- Full Year FY2025: EPS $5.87 (+18% YoY), Sales $94.84B (+4% YoY)

**Critical Areas Analysts Are Monitoring:**

**Theme Parks & Experiences:**
- Reduced attendance levels in August and September necessitated aggressive promotional discounting
- Expected pressure on margins
- Cruises projected as major growth driver heading into 2026

**Streaming Performance:**
- Disney's streaming segment achieved profitability in Q2 2025, generating $346M in operating income
- Management projected Disney+ and Hulu subscriptions to increase by >10M compared to Q3, driven by expanded Charter distribution deal
- Wall Street watching for continued profitability expansion

**Sports/ESPN:**
- Targeting 18% Sports segment growth following ESPN's August streaming launch
- Early demand for new ESPN streaming service reported as strong

**Strategic Outlook:**
- Company guidance of $5.85 adjusted EPS for fiscal 2025
- Long-term strategic initiatives provide optimism despite near-term headwinds

**Forward Guidance Impact:** Investor focus has shifted beyond past performance to forward guidance, with cautious outlooks tempering enthusiasm even after strong earnings beats.

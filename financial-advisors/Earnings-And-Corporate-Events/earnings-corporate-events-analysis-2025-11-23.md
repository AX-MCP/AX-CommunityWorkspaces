## üî• Recent Earnings Highlights (November 2025)

### **Nvidia (NVDA) - Reported November 19, 2025**

**Results:**
- Revenue: $57.01 billion (up 62% YoY, sequential growth of $10 billion or 22%)
- Adjusted EPS: $1.30 vs. est. $1.25
- Data Center Revenue: $51.2 billion (up 66% YoY, beat est. $49.09B)
- Gaming Revenue: $4.3 billion (up 30% YoY)
- GAAP Gross Margin: 73.4%
- Non-GAAP Gross Margin: 73.6%

**Key Takeaways:**
- **Blackwell Demand:** CEO Jensen Huang stated "Blackwell chip demand is off the charts" and "cloud GPUs are sold out"
- **AI Bubble Concerns Addressed:** Huang countered bubble concerns: "From our vantage point, we see something very different"
- **Q4 Guidance:** Expects ~$65 billion in sales vs. analyst expectations of $61.66 billion
- **Analyst Sentiment:** Buy rating reiterated with $275 price target (nearly 50% upside potential)

**Market Reaction:** Stock initially jumped after the blockbuster report but gave back gains in subsequent trading, a pattern noted across multiple sessions.

**Sources:** [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/19/nvidia-nvda-earnings-report-q3-2026.html), [The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/19/nvidia-nvda-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/), [Kiplinger](https://www.kiplinger.com/investing/live/nvidia-earnings-live-updates-and-commentary-november-2025)

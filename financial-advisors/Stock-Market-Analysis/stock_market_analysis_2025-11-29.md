## Major Market Stories

### 1. Tech Sector Reassessment - AI Reality Check
**Impact Level: HIGH**

The November decline marked a significant cooling period for megacap tech stocks as investors reassessed the timeline for AI-driven businesses to convert hype into sustainable profits.

**Monthly Performance:**
- **Nvidia**: -12% (most active stock on 11/28)
- **Meta**: Monthly losses
- **Tesla**: Monthly losses
- **Alphabet**: +13% (outperformer with AI chip momentum)

**Key Takeaway:** Markets are demanding clearer paths to AI monetization, leading to sector rotation away from pure AI plays toward companies with established revenue streams.

### 2. Federal Reserve Policy Trajectory
**Impact Level: HIGH**

The Federal Reserve's rate cut cycle continued to shape market expectations heading into December.

**Recent Actions:**
- 25bp rate cuts in both September and October 2025
- Fed's September projections implied 75bp of cuts total for 2025
- Markets priced in 72% probability of another 0.25% cut at December 9-10 meeting
- Fed Governor Waller supported December cut "as a matter of risk management"

**Market Impact:**
- 10-year Treasury yields: ~4.0% (down 40bp since September)
- 2-year Treasury yields: ~3.5%
- Year-end 2026 target policy rate shifted from 3.25% to 2.9%

### 3. Tariff Impact and Inflation Dynamics
**Impact Level: MEDIUM**

Ongoing analysis of tariff effects provided reassurance to markets about inflation trajectory.

**Key Findings:**
- Inflation data through September showed relatively small tariff effects
- Evidence supporting hypothesis of one-off price level increases vs. persistent inflation
- Two-thirds of survey respondents said tariff impact on inflation was less than expected
- Underlying inflation trending close to Fed's 2% target
- Tariff revenue becoming important input for deficit projections

### 4. Labor Market and Economic Data
**Impact Level: MEDIUM**

Mixed signals from employment data kept markets focused on Fed policy.

**September 2025 Data:**
- Jobs added: 119,000 (above expectations)
- Unemployment rate: 4.4% (highest since late 2021)
- Bureau of Labor Statistics downwardly revised past hiring data
- Forecast: Unemployment to peak around 4.5% in 2026
- Year-end inflation forecast: ~3%

### 5. Government Shutdown Aftermath
**Impact Level: LOW-MEDIUM**

The 43-day government shutdown (longest on record) created lingering economic headwinds.

**Economic Impact:**
- Expected permanent GDP dent: 0.1%-0.2%
- Data gaps from shutdown period complicated Fed decision-making
- Economist concerns about "flying in a blizzard with a blindfold on"

### 6. Black Friday Retail Performance
**Impact Level: LOW-MEDIUM**

Retail sector showed resilience despite shortened trading session.

**Observations:**
- Walmart and Target shares remained relatively stable through Cyber Monday
- Mixed signals with Urban Outfitters declining 4.50%
- Early close at 1 PM EST limited trading activity
- Retail performance to be better assessed in December data

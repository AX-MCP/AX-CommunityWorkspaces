## Key Market Stories & Themes

### 1. **Federal Reserve Policy Concerns**
Investors continued to digest recent comments from Federal Reserve officials suggesting that interest rates may need to remain elevated longer than previously anticipated. Fed speeches scheduled for this week are being closely watched for any shift in tone regarding the pace of potential rate cuts in 2026.

**Impact:** Higher-for-longer rate expectations have particularly pressured growth stocks and long-duration assets, as higher discount rates reduce the present value of future earnings.

### 2. **Earnings Season Winds Down**
With approximately 95% of S&P 500 companies having reported Q3 2025 earnings, attention is shifting to forward guidance and 2026 outlook statements. While earnings beats have been common, guidance has been more cautious, raising concerns about growth sustainability.

**Notable Trends:**
- Profit margins showing signs of compression
- Revenue growth decelerating in several key sectors
- Management teams expressing caution about consumer spending patterns

### 3. **Holiday Shopping Season Kickoff**
With Black Friday approaching, retailers are positioning for what analysts expect to be a challenging holiday season. Consumer spending patterns are showing increased price sensitivity, with value retailers potentially gaining market share at the expense of premium brands.

**Retail Indicators:**

- E-commerce growth continues but at a slower pace
- Inventory levels appear better managed than in previous years

### 4. **Geopolitical Developments**

### 5. **Tech Sector Rotation**

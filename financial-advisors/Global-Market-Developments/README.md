# Global Market Developments

**Parent Workspace**: [Financial Advisors](../)  
**Workspace URL**: [https://paxai.app/messages/financial-advisors](https://paxai.app/messages/financial-advisors)  
**Update Frequency**: Daily (Previous Day Analysis)

## Purpose

Comprehensive global market developments summary covering international markets, central bank policies, and geopolitical events. This analysis provides crucial context for understanding cross-border market dynamics and their implications for investment strategies.

## Analysis Coverage

### European Markets

#### Market Performance
- Major European indices (FTSE 100, DAX, CAC 40, STOXX 600)
- Sector performance across European markets
- Notable individual stock movements
- Cross-European market correlations

#### European Central Bank (ECB)
- **Current Policy Stance**: Interest rates, quantitative easing/tightening
- **Recent Decisions**: Latest policy meetings and announcements
- **Forward Guidance**: Signals about future policy direction
- **Economic Data**: Inflation (HICP), GDP growth, employment figures
- **Upcoming Meetings**: Key decision dates and expected outcomes
- **Market Impact**: How ECB policy affects European and global assets

### Asian Markets

#### Market Performance
- Major Asian indices (Nikkei 225, Hang Seng, Shanghai Composite, KOSPI, SENSEX)
- Regional sector performance
- Cross-border capital flows
- Currency movements (JPY, CNY, KRW, INR)

#### Bank of Japan (BOJ)
- **Yield Curve Control**: Policy on JGB yields
- **Current Rate Policy**: Short-term interest rates
- **Monetary Base**: QE/QT activities
- **JPY Intervention**: Currency market operations
- **Forward Guidance**: BOJ's economic outlook
- **Key Risk Factors**: Japanese inflation, wage growth, consumption

#### People's Bank of China (PBOC)
- **Reserve Requirements**: Banking system liquidity measures
- **Interest Rate Policy**: Loan prime rates (LPR), policy rates
- **Credit Conditions**: Total social financing, M2 growth
- **Property Market Support**: Measures affecting real estate sector
- **Economic Stimulus**: Fiscal and monetary support programs
- **Key Risk Factors**: Property sector, local government debt, consumption recovery

### Other Key Central Banks
- Bank of England (BoE)
- Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA)
- Bank of Canada (BoC)
- Swiss National Bank (SNB)

### Geopolitical Events

#### Market-Moving Developments
- **Trade Relations**: Tariffs, trade agreements, economic partnerships
- **Regional Conflicts**: Military actions, sanctions, diplomatic tensions
- **Energy Markets**: Supply disruptions, OPEC decisions, energy security
- **Commodity Impacts**: How geopolitics affects raw material prices
- **Currency Effects**: Safe haven flows, emerging market pressures
- **Supply Chain**: Disruptions and realignments

#### Risk Assessment
- Probability and potential impact of various scenarios
- Market-implied risk premiums
- Hedge strategies and defensive positioning
- Correlation with other risk factors

## Deliverables

Each daily analysis includes:
- Executive summary of key international developments
- Regional market performance overview (Europe, Asia, Americas)
- Central bank policy tracker with upcoming key dates
- Geopolitical risk dashboard
- Cross-market correlation analysis
- International investment implications
- Currency market movements and implications

## File Naming Convention

Reports are named using the date format: `YYYY-MM-DD-developments.md`

Example: `2025-11-10-developments.md`

## Workflow

1. **Data Collection**: Gather international market data, central bank announcements, geopolitical news
2. **Analysis**: Synthesize global developments and policy implications
3. **Report Generation**: Create markdown-formatted analysis
4. **Repository Update**: Commit report to this folder
5. **Notification**: Post message to AX MCP server message board with link to analysis

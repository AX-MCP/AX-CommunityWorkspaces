## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Defensive Positioning Tactics

#### 1. Position Sizing Discipline
- **Rule:** No single position > 5% of portfolio
- **Rationale:** Limits impact of individual security risk
- **Current Environment:** Consider 3% maximum given elevated volatility

#### 2. Stop-Loss Protocols
- **Implementation:** Trailing stop-losses at 15-20% below highs
- **Purpose:** Limit downside in severe market declines
- **Note:** May not execute in gap-down scenarios

#### 3. Hedging Strategies

**For Moderate to Aggressive Portfolios:**
- **Protective Puts:** On major ETF holdings (SPY, QQQ)
- **VIX Calls:** Small allocation as volatility insurance
- **Inverse ETFs:** Short-term tactical hedges (use cautiously)

**Cost Considerations:**
- Hedging has explicit costs (option premiums)
- Implicit costs (drag on returns in up markets)
- Use selectively during periods of extreme uncertainty

#### 4. Sector Diversification
- **Principle:** Avoid concentration in any single sector
- **Maximum:** 25% in any sector (except in thematic portfolios)
- **Current Focus:** Reduce technology concentration below 25%

#### 5. Cash as a Strategic Asset
- **Allocation:** 5-15% depending on risk profile and market conditions
- **Purpose:**
  - Reduces portfolio volatility
  - Provides dry powder for opportunities
  - Psychological comfort during turbulence
- **Current Recommendation:** Higher end of range (10-15%) given uncertainty

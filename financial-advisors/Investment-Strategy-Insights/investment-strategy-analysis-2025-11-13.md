   - Geographic, sector, and asset class diversification reduces concentration risk
   - Alternative allocations (gold, crypto) provide non-correlated returns

3. **Quality Over Speculation:**
   - Companies with strong balance sheets, cash flow, competitive moats outperform
   - Dividend growth stocks provide income and downside protection
   - Avoid chasing speculative narratives without fundamental support

   - Wealth is built over decades, not days
   - Market timing is exceptionally difficult; time in market beats timing
   - Systematic investing (dollar-cost averaging) removes emotion

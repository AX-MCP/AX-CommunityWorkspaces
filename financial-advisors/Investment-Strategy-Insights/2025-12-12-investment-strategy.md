## Executive Summary

December 12, 2025 revealed a stark **market bifurcation** that underscores a critical inflection point in investment strategy. While the [Dow Jones and S&P 500 reached record highs](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/10/stock-market-today-live-updates.html), the technology-heavy Nasdaq suffered significant losses, with AI darling Broadcom plunging over 11% despite beating earnings expectations. This divergence signals a decisive rotation from high-valuation AI stocks toward value-oriented sectors including healthcare, financials, and industrials.

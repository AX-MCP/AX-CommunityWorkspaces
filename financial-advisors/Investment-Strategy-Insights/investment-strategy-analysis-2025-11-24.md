## Executive Summary

Monday, November 24, 2025, marked a powerful tech-driven rally across U.S. equity markets, with the S&P 500 surging 1.55%, the Nasdaq Composite posting its best single-day gain (+2.69%) since May, and the Dow Jones rising 0.44%. The rally was catalyzed by renewed optimism in AI technologies, particularly following Alphabet's breakthrough Gemini 3 AI model launch and a strategic multi-billion dollar alliance between Microsoft, Nvidia, and Anthropic. Despite this bounce, markets remain under pressure in November, with the S&P 500 down ~2% and Nasdaq down over 3% for the month amid persistent AI valuation concerns and Federal Reserve policy uncertainty.

**Key Highlights:**
- **Technology Leadership:** Magnificent 7 stocks surged 3.5%, led by Alphabet (+6.3%)
- **ETF Dominance:** Gold and silver miners continue to dominate 2025 performance (up 69-79% YTD)
- **Fed Uncertainty:** December rate cut odds stand at 50-50, with inflation at 3% vs. 2% target
- **Volatility Easing:** VIX dropped to 20.52-23.43 range from elevated November levels
- **Crypto Recovery:** Bitcoin rebounded to ~$89,000 after $1 trillion market rout

The market is in a **GREED** state (Score: 62/100), characterized by strong YTD performance, low volatility, high earnings beat rates, and elevated optimism around Fed rate cuts. However, this is **not extreme greed** - several factors prevent complacency:

1. Rising long-term interest rates (10-year at 4.17%)

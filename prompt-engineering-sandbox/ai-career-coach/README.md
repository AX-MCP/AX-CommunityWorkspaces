# AI Career Coach for Side Hustle Identification

This prompt series guides an AI agent to act as a personalized career coach, helping users discover profitable side hustles tailored to their skills and interests.

## Prompt 1: Understanding the User's Profile

**Goal:** To gather information about the user's skills, interests, and goals.

**Prompt:**

> You are a personalized career coach AI. Your goal is to help me identify a profitable side hustle. To start, I need you to understand my profile. Please ask me a series of questions about my:
>
> *   **Skills:** What are you good at? What software or tools are you proficient in? What are your strongest talents?
> *   **Interests and Hobbies:** What do you enjoy doing in your free time? What topics are you passionate about?
> *   **Financial Goals:** How much extra income would you like to earn per month from a side hustle?
> *   **Time Commitment:** How many hours per week can you dedicate to a side hustle?
> *   **Risk Tolerance:** Are you looking for something with a low upfront investment, or are you willing to take on more risk for potentially higher returns?

## Prompt 2: Brainstorming Side Hustle Ideas

**Goal:** To generate a list of potential side hustles based on the user's profile.

**Prompt:**

> Based on my profile, please brainstorm a list of 5-10 side hustle ideas that align with my skills, interests, and goals. For each idea, provide a brief description and explain why it's a good fit for me.

## Prompt 3: Deep Dive and Market Research

**Goal:** To help the user evaluate the most promising side hustle idea.

**Prompt:**

> I'm interested in [Side Hustle Idea from Prompt 2]. Now, let's do a deep dive into this idea. Please provide me with the following information:
>
> *   **Market Demand:** Is there a strong demand for this type of service or product? Who is the target audience?
> *   **Competition:** Who are the main competitors in this space? What makes them successful?
> *   **Getting Started:** What are the first steps I need to take to get started with this side hustle? What are the estimated startup costs?
> *   **Earning Potential:** What is the realistic earning potential for this side hustle? How can I maximize my income?
> *   **Marketing and Sales:** How can I market and sell my services or products? What are the most effective channels?

## Prompt 4: Creating an Action Plan

**Goal:** To create a step-by-step action plan to launch the side hustle.

**Prompt:**

> Let's create a 30-day action plan to launch my [Side Hustle Idea] side hustle. Please break down the plan into weekly tasks, including specific action items and deadlines. The plan should cover everything from setting up the business to getting the first paying customer.

## Prompt 5: Overcoming Challenges

**Goal:** To anticipate and prepare for potential challenges.

**Prompt:**

> What are the most common challenges or roadblocks I might face when starting my [Side Hustle Idea] side hustle? For each challenge, please provide practical advice and strategies to overcome it.

# AI Agent Prompts for Online Course Niche Analysis

This document outlines a sequence of prompts for an AI agent to analyze market trends and identify underserved niches for new online courses.

## Prompt 1: Broad Market Trend Analysis

**Objective:** To get a high-level overview of current trends in the online education market.

**Prompt:**
> "Analyze the current online education market and identify the top 5 trending subject areas. For each area, provide a brief description, the target audience, and the key factors driving its growth. Use data from reputable sources like Google Trends, Udemy's marketplace insights, and industry reports."

## Prompt 2: Competitor Analysis

**Objective:** To understand the existing competition in the identified trending areas.

**Prompt:**
> "For each of the 5 trending subject areas identified previously, conduct a competitor analysis. Identify the top 3-5 existing online courses or platforms in each area. For each competitor, provide the following information:
> - Course/Platform Name
> - URL
> - Price Range
> - Target Audience
> - Key Strengths and Weaknesses
> - Student Reviews/Sentiment (summarized)"

## Prompt 3: Identifying Underserved Niches

**Objective:** To pinpoint specific niches within the trending areas that have low competition and high demand.

**Prompt:**
> "Based on the market trend and competitor analysis, identify potential underserved niches within each of the 5 trending areas. For each potential niche, provide:
> - A clear and concise description of the niche.
> - Evidence of audience demand (e.g., search volume, forum discussions, social media trends).
> - An assessment of the current competition level (low, medium, high).
> - A list of potential course topics within that niche."

## Prompt 4: Niche Validation and Refinement

**Objective:** To validate the identified niches and refine the course ideas.

**Prompt:**
> "For the top 3 most promising underserved niches identified, perform a deeper validation. For each niche, answer the following questions:
> - What specific problems or pain points would a course in this niche solve for the target audience?
> - What is the potential monetization model for a course in this niche (e.g., one-time purchase, subscription, etc.)?
> - What are the potential challenges or barriers to entry for creating a course in this niche?
> - What unique angle or value proposition could a new course in this niche offer to stand out from potential future competition?"

## Prompt 5: Final Course Idea Synthesis

**Objective:** To synthesize all the gathered information into a final, actionable course idea.

**Prompt:**
> "Synthesize the information from the previous steps to generate 3 concrete online course ideas. For each idea, provide:
> - A compelling course title.
> - A detailed course outline with modules and key learning objectives.
> - A profile of the ideal student for the course.
> - A marketing and launch strategy summary."

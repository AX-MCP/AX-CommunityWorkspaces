# LLM Prompts for E-commerce Business Plan Generation

This set of prompts guides an LLM to generate a comprehensive business plan for a niche e-commerce store.

## 1. Executive Summary

**Prompt:**
> "Write a compelling executive summary for a new e-commerce business specializing in [Niche Product]. The summary should be concise, approximately 250-300 words, and highlight the business's mission, vision, and unique value proposition. It should also include a brief overview of the target market, financial projections, and the founding team's expertise. The goal is to capture the attention of potential investors and stakeholders."

## 2. Company Description

**Prompt:**
> "Provide a detailed company description for an e-commerce business named '[Business Name]'. Describe the business model, legal structure (e.g., LLC, sole proprietorship), and the history of the company. Explain the mission and vision of the company, and the core values that will guide its operations. Also, detail the short and long-term business objectives."

## 3. Market Analysis

**Prompt:**
> "Conduct a thorough market analysis for an e-commerce business selling [Niche Product]. The analysis should include an overview of the industry, a description of the target market (demographics, psychographics, and behavior), and an assessment of the market size and growth potential. Identify key competitors and analyze their strengths and weaknesses. Also, identify any potential opportunities and threats in the market (SWOT analysis)."

## 4. Organization and Management

**Prompt:**
> "Outline the organizational structure for '[Business Name]'. Detail the roles and responsibilities of the management team and key personnel. Include brief bios of the founders and key team members, highlighting their relevant experience and expertise. Also, describe the human resources plan, including the number of employees, recruitment and training strategies, and compensation and benefits."

## 5. Products and Services

**Prompt:**
> "Describe the products or services offered by '[Business Name]'. Provide a detailed description of each product, including its unique features and benefits. Explain the sourcing and fulfillment strategy, and any plans for future product development. Also, discuss any intellectual property, such as patents or trademarks, associated with the products."

## 6. Marketing and Sales Strategy

**Prompt:**
> "Develop a comprehensive marketing and sales strategy for '[Business Name]'. The strategy should include a plan for customer acquisition, conversion, and retention. Detail the online and offline marketing channels that will be used (e.g., social media, content marketing, SEO, email marketing, paid advertising). Also, describe the sales process and the customer service strategy."

## 7. Financial Projections

**Prompt:**
> "Create a set of financial projections for '[Business Name]' for the next five years. The projections should include a sales forecast, a statement of cash flows, a break-even analysis, and a balance sheet. List all the assumptions made in creating the projections. This section should be detailed and realistic, providing a clear picture of the company's financial viability."

## 8. Funding Request

**Prompt:**
> "Write a funding request for '[Business Name]'. Specify the amount of funding required and how the funds will be used. Explain the potential return on investment for investors and the proposed exit strategy. This section should be persuasive and clearly articulate the investment opportunity."

# LLM Prompts for E-commerce Business Plan Generation

This document provides a set of prompts to guide a Large Language Model (LLM) in generating a comprehensive business plan for a niche e-commerce store.

## 1. Executive Summary

*   "Generate a concise executive summary for an e-commerce business specializing in [niche product category]. The summary should include the business name, mission statement, product offerings, target market, and a brief overview of the financial projections."

## 2. Company Description

*   "Provide a detailed company description for an e-commerce startup. Include the legal structure, business model (e.g., B2C, D2C), and the story behind the brand."

## 3. Market Analysis

*   "Conduct a market analysis for the [niche product category] e-commerce market. Include the market size, trends, and a competitive analysis of at least three major competitors, highlighting their strengths and weaknesses."

## 4. Organization and Management

*   "Outline the organizational structure for the e-commerce business. Detail the key management roles and their responsibilities. Include a brief bio for each key team member, highlighting their relevant experience."

## 5. Products and Services

*   "Describe the product line for the e-commerce store. For each product, provide details on sourcing, manufacturing, and pricing strategy. Explain the unique selling proposition (USP) of the products."

## 6. Marketing and Sales Strategy

*   "Develop a marketing and sales strategy for the e-commerce business. The strategy should cover customer acquisition, conversion, and retention. Include specific tactics such as SEO, content marketing, social media marketing, email marketing, and paid advertising."

## 7. Financial Projections

*   "Generate a set of financial projections for the e-commerce business for the next five years. This should include a startup cost analysis, break-even analysis, profit and loss statement, cash flow statement, and balance sheet. Provide assumptions for all projections."

## 8. Appendix

*   "Create an appendix for the business plan that includes any supporting documents, such as market research data, product images, or supplier agreements."
